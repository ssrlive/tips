# 在 Windows 的 VMWare 虚拟机里安装 macOS Tahoe

## Requirements
- Virtualization Enabled: Intel VT-x or AMD-V must be enabled in your BIOS/UEFI. Please refer to online guides.
- RAM: Minimum `8GB` (`16GB` or more recommended for smooth performance).
- Storage: At least `80GB` of free space for the virtual machine.
- VMware Workstation Pro: Version 25H2 is used in this guide.
- Required Files: All downloads are available from the provided links. Ensure you have them ready.
